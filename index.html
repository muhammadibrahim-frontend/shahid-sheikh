Who Am I?

I'm Shahid Sheikh, a UK-based business consultant with over 35 years of experience helping companies achieve their growth objectives. My journey began in London where I started my first business at the age of 25, and since then I've worked with over 200 clients across Europe and North America.

My approach combines data-driven analysis with creative problem-solving to deliver tailored solutions for each client. I believe in building long-term relationships and seeing businesses thrive beyond our initial engagement.
